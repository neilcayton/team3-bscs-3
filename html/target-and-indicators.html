Target 11.1

Ensure safe, adequate, and affordable housing and basic services for all by 2030. Upgrade slums to improve living conditions.

Target 11.2

Promote safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable public transport systems by 2030, with emphasis on vulnerable groups.

Target 11.3

Enhance inclusive and sustainable urbanization and human settlement planning by 2030 through participatory processes.

Target 11.4

Strengthen efforts to protect and preserve cultural and natural heritage worldwide.

Target 11.5

Reduce deaths, affected people, and economic losses due to disasters by 2030, focusing on vulnerable groups.

Target 11.6

Reduce the per capita environmental impact of cities by 2030, focusing on air quality and waste management.

Target 11.7

Ensure universal access to safe, inclusive, and accessible green and public spaces, particularly for vulnerable groups.

Target 11.a

Enhance positive economic, social, and environmental links between urban, peri-urban, and rural areas through development planning.

Target 11.b

Increase cities adopting integrated policies on inclusion, resource efficiency, climate change mitigation, and disaster risk management by 2020.

Target 11.c

Support the least developed countries in building sustainable and resilient structures using local materials through financial and technical assistance.
